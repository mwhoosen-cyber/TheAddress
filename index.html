Our Story

Where Every Meal
Tells a Story

The Address is more than a restaurant — it's a destination. Nestled in the heart of Durban, from the moment you arrive, you are welcomed into a world of bold, international flavours crafted with care and served with pride.

Our menu is a culinary journey across the globe: from the smoky grills of Lahore to the sun-drenched prawn platters of Mauritius, from the sizzling steakhouses of Madrid to the vibrant street food of Miami. Every dish is a story.

Proudly halaal, we cater to families, corporate events, and every occasion in between. Whether it's a Friday Jummah buffet or an intimate dinner, The Address delivers — right here in Durban.
